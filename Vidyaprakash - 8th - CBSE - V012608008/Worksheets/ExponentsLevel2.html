Exponents and Powers - Level 2 (Standard)
Especially designed for: Aarav Class: 8 CBSE Subject: Mathematics
Section A: Advanced Simplifications
1.
Simplify: $\frac{25 \times t^{-4}}{5^{-3} \times 10 \times t^{-8}} \ (t \neq 0)$
2.
Simplify: $\frac{3^{-5} \times 10^{-5} \times 125}{5^{-7} \times 6^{-5}}$
3.
Simplify: $\left\{ \left( \frac{1}{3} \right)^{-1} - \left( \frac{1}{4} \right)^{-1} \right\}^{-1}$
4.
Simplify: $\left(\frac{5}{8}\right)^{-7} \times \left(\frac{8}{5}\right)^{-4}$
5.
By what number should $(-4)^{-1}$ be multiplied so that the product becomes $10^{-1}$?
Section B: Find the Unknown Variable
6.
Find the value of $x$ if: $\left(\frac{2}{5}\right)^{-3} \times \left(\frac{2}{5}\right)^{15} = \left(\frac{2}{5}\right)^{2 + 3x}$
7.
Find $m$ so that $(-3)^{m+1} \times (-3)^5 = (-3)^7$
8.
If $5^{2x+1} \div 25 = 125$, find the value of $x$.
9.
Find $x$ if $2^x \times 2^x = 4^x$. Is this true for all $x$? Briefly explain.
10.
Find the value of $p$ if $p = \left( \frac{-2}{5} \right)^2 \div \left( \frac{-2}{5} \right)^3$.
Section C: Application & Comparisons
11.
Compare the following: $2.7 \times 10^{12}$ and $1.5 \times 10^8$.
12.
Compare the size of a red blood cell which is $0.000007 \text{ m}$ to a plant cell which is $0.0000129 \text{ m}$.
13.
In a stack there are $5$ books each of thickness $20 \text{ mm}$ and $5$ paper sheets each of thickness $0.016 \text{ mm}$. What is the total thickness of the stack in standard form?
14.
Mass of Earth is $5.97 \times 10^{24} \text{ kg}$ and mass of Moon is $7.35 \times 10^{22} \text{ kg}$. What is their total mass?
15.
The distance between Sun and Earth is $1.496 \times 10^{11} \text{ m}$ and the distance between Earth and Moon is $3.84 \times 10^8 \text{ m}$. During a solar eclipse, the Moon comes in between Earth and Sun. At that time what is the distance between Moon and Sun?
Answer Key
1.
Ans: $\frac{5^2 \times t^{-4}}{5^{-3} \times (2 \times 5) \times t^{-8}} = \frac{5^2 \times t^{-4}}{5^{-2} \times 2 \times t^{-8}} = \frac{5^{2 - (-2)} \times t^{-4 - (-8)}}{2} = \frac{625 t^4}{2}$
2.
Ans: $\frac{3^{-5} \times (2 \times 5)^{-5} \times 5^3}{5^{-7} \times (2 \times 3)^{-5}} = \frac{3^{-5} \times 2^{-5} \times 5^{-5} \times 5^3}{5^{-7} \times 2^{-5} \times 3^{-5}} = 5^{-2} \div 5^{-7} = 5^5 = 3125$
3.
Ans: $\{ 3 - 4 \}^{-1} = \{-1\}^{-1} = -1$
4.
Ans: $\left(\frac{5}{8}\right)^{-7} \times \left(\frac{5}{8}\right)^4 = \left(\frac{5}{8}\right)^{-3} = \left(\frac{8}{5}\right)^3 = \frac{512}{125}$
5.
Ans: Let number be $x$. $(-\frac{1}{4}) \times x = \frac{1}{10} \implies x = \frac{1}{10} \times (-4) = -\frac{2}{5}$
6.
Ans: $-3 + 15 = 2 + 3x \implies 12 = 2 + 3x \implies 3x = 10 \implies x = \frac{10}{3}$
7.
Ans: $m + 1 + 5 = 7 \implies m + 6 = 7 \implies m = 1$
8.
Ans: $\frac{5^{2x+1}}{5^2} = 5^3 \implies 5^{2x-1} = 5^3 \implies 2x - 1 = 3 \implies 2x = 4 \implies x = 2$
9.
Ans: $2^{2x} = 2^{2x}$. Yes, it is an identity true for all real values of $x$.
10.
Ans: $p = \left( \frac{-2}{5} \right)^{2-3} = \left( \frac{-2}{5} \right)^{-1} = \frac{-5}{2}$
11.
Ans: $2.7 \times 10^{12} > 1.5 \times 10^8$ (Higher exponent indicates larger number).
12.
Ans: RBC = $7 \times 10^{-6}$, Plant Cell = $12.9 \times 10^{-6}$. Plant cell is larger.
13.
Ans: $5 \times 20 + 5 \times 0.016 = 100 + 0.080 = 100.08 \text{ mm} = 1.0008 \times 10^2 \text{ mm}$
14.
Ans: $(597 \times 10^{22}) + (7.35 \times 10^{22}) = 604.35 \times 10^{22} = 6.0435 \times 10^{24} \text{ kg}$
15.
Ans: Distance = $(1.496 \times 10^{11}) - (0.00384 \times 10^{11}) = 1.49216 \times 10^{11} \text{ m}$

Reason (R): Villi secrete hydrochloric acid to kill bacteria in the food.
31.
Assertion (A): Exhaled air is warmer and misty, especially visible during winter.
Reason (R): Exhaled air is saturated with water vapour and adopts the body's internal temperature.
32.

33.

Reason (R): Millions of small alveoli tremendously increase the surface area for the rapid exchange of gases.

Reason (R): The oesophagus only acts as a passage pushing food via peristalsis.

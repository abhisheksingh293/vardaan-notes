Cubes & Cube Roots - Level 3 (Challenger)
Designed for: Arnav Mishra Class: 8 CBSE Total Questions: 20
Section A: Complex Evaluations (Q1 - Q7)
1.
Evaluate: $\sqrt[3]{0.000064}$
2.
Evaluate: $\sqrt[3]{1000} + \sqrt[3]{0.008} - \sqrt[3]{0.125}$
3.
Evaluate: $\sqrt[3]{0.027 \times 0.008}$
4.
Simplify: $\sqrt[3]{\frac{-512}{343}} \times \sqrt[3]{\frac{-729}{1331}}$
5.
Find the value of $\sqrt[3]{4 \frac{12}{125}}$.
6.
Evaluate: $\sqrt[3]{288 \times \sqrt[3]{72 \times \sqrt[3]{27}}}$
7.
Without finding the exact value, determine the number of digits in the cube root of a 8-digit perfect cube.
Section B: Equations & Variables (Q8 - Q14)
8.
If $\sqrt[3]{x} = -4$, find the value of $x$.
9.
If $x^3 = \frac{216}{42875}$, find $x$.
10.
Solve for $x$: $\sqrt[3]{1 + \frac{x}{8}} = \frac{3}{2}$
11.
If the cube of a number $y$ is $50.653$, find the value of $y$.
12.
If $\sqrt[3]{a} + \sqrt[3]{125} = 8$, find the value of $a$.
13.
If $\sqrt[3]{3^x} = 27$, find the value of $x$.
14.
Find the value of $\frac{\sqrt[3]{0.729} - \sqrt[3]{0.343}}{\sqrt[3]{0.729} + \sqrt[3]{0.343}}$.
Section C: Advanced Word Problems & HOTS (Q15 - Q20)
15.
The volume of a cubical box is $13.824 \text{ m}^3$. Find the total surface area of the box.
16.

17.
Evaluate $\sqrt[3]{(4^3 - 3^3) \times (3^3 - 2^3)}$.
18.
A solid cubical block of wood costs ₹256 at the rate of ₹500 per cubic metre. Find the length of its edge.
19.
How many small cubes of edge $2 \text{ cm}$ can be cut from a cubical block of edge $10 \text{ cm}$?
20.
Find the difference between the cubes of the numbers 11 and 12, without actually computing the cubes directly. (Hint: Use pattern $n^3 - (n-1)^3$).

Exponents and Powers - Level 1 (Foundation)
Especially designed for: Aarav Class: 8 CBSE Subject: Mathematics
Section A: Evaluate the Following
1.
Evaluate: $3^{-2}$
2.
Evaluate: $(-4)^{-2}$
3.
Evaluate: $\left(\frac{1}{2}\right)^{-5}$
4.
Find the value of $2^0 + 3^0 + 4^0$.
5.
Evaluate: $\left(\frac{3}{4}\right)^{-2}$
Section B: Simplify using Laws of Exponents
6.
Simplify and express with a positive exponent: $(-4)^5 \div (-4)^8$
7.
Simplify: $\left(\frac{1}{2^3}\right)^2$
8.
Simplify: $(-3)^4 \times \left(\frac{5}{3}\right)^4$
9.
Simplify: $(3^{-7} \div 3^{-10}) \times 3^{-5}$
10.

Section C: Standard Form and Usual Form
11.
Express $0.000000564$ in standard form.
12.
Express $21600000$ in standard form.
13.
Express the number appearing in the statement in standard form: Size of a plant cell is $0.00001275 \text{ m}$.
14.
Express $3.02 \times 10^{-6}$ in usual form.
15.
Express $4.5 \times 10^4$ in usual form.
Section D: Mixed Basic Problems
16.
Find the value of $(3^0 + 4^{-1}) \times 2^2$.
17.
Find the value of $(2^{-1} \times 4^{-1}) \div 2^{-2}$.
18.
Find the value of $\left(\frac{1}{2}\right)^{-2} + \left(\frac{1}{3}\right)^{-2} + \left(\frac{1}{4}\right)^{-2}$.
19.
Find the multiplicative inverse of $10^{-5}$.
20.
Find the value of $m$ for which $5^m \div 5^{-3} = 5^5$.

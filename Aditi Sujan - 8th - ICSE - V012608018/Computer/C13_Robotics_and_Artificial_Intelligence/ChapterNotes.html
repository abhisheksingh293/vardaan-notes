Robotics and Artificial Intelligence

Concept

Robotics deals with designing and using robots, while AI gives machines the ability to think and decide intelligently.

1. What is a Robot?

A robot is a machine that can perform tasks automatically or semi-automatically. Some robots follow fixed instructions, while smart robots can sense and react to the environment.

2. Parts of a Robot

  • Sensors collect information.
  • Controller processes the information.
  • Actuators perform the action.
  • Power source runs the robot.
Fact

Robots are used in hospitals, industries, space exploration, and homes.

3. Relationship Between Robotics and AI

AI helps robots become smarter by allowing them to learn, recognize patterns, and make better decisions.

Important

Not every robot has AI, but many advanced robots use AI techniques.

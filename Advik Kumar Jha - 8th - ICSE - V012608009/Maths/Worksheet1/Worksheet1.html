Squares, Cubes & Their Roots - Foundation Level 1
Especially designed for: Advik Class: 8 (ICSE) Subject: Mathematics
Section A: Properties of Squares
1.
Find the square of the number $15$.
2.
Find the square of the number $25$.
3.
Without calculating, state whether $1057$ is a perfect square or not. Give a reason for your answer.
4.
What will be the unit digit of the square of the number $539$?
5.
What will be the unit digit of the square of the number $731$?
6.
How many natural numbers lie strictly between $9^2$ and $10^2$?
7.
Express the perfect square $49$ as the sum of $7$ consecutive odd numbers.
8.
Calculate the square of the decimal number $1.2$.
9.
Find the square of the fraction $\frac{3}{7}$.
10.
Identify which of the following numbers is the square of an even number: $121$, $225$, $256$, or $6561$.
11.
Identify which of the following numbers is the square of an odd number: $400$, $169$, $196$, or $900$.
12.
Find the square of the negative integer $-11$.
13.
Is $90$ a perfect square? Use the prime factorization method to justify your answer.
14.
Find the smallest whole number by which $48$ must be multiplied so as to get a perfect square number.
15.
Evaluate the expression: $10^2 - 9^2$ without actually squaring the numbers.
Section B: Calculating Square Roots
16.
Find the square root of $196$ using the method of repeated subtraction.
17.
Find the square root of $729$ by the prime factorization method.
18.
Find the square root of $400$ by the prime factorization method.
19.
Find the square root of $1764$.
20.
Find the smallest whole number by which $252$ must be divided to get a perfect square.
21.
Find the square root of $529$ using the long division method.
22.
Find the square root of $1024$ using the long division method.
23.
Find the square root of the decimal number $12.25$.
24.
Find the square root of the decimal number $42.25$.
25.
Find the square root of the fraction $\frac{144}{169}$.
26.
Evaluate the square root: $\sqrt{0.0009}$.
27.
A square garden has an area of $900\text{ m}^2$. Find the length of its side.
28.
If $\sqrt{x} = 16$, what is the value of $x$?
29.
Calculate the sum: $\sqrt{25} + \sqrt{36}$.
30.
Evaluate the expression: $\sqrt{100 - 64}$.
31.
Evaluate the expression: $\sqrt{100} - \sqrt{64}$. (Is it the same as the previous question?)
32.
Find the length of the diagonal of a square whose area is $50\text{ cm}^2$.
33.
There are $2025$ plants to be planted in a garden in such a way that each row contains as many plants as the total number of rows. Find the number of rows.
34.
Find the smallest square number that is perfectly divisible by each of the numbers $4$, $9$, and $10$.
35.
Estimate the value of $\sqrt{80}$ to the nearest whole number.
Section C: Properties of Cubes

37.
Find the cube of the number $11$.

39.

42.

Section D: Calculating Cube Roots
46.
Find the cube root of $125$.
47.
Find the cube root of $512$ by the prime factorization method.
48.
Find the cube root of $3375$ by the prime factorization method.
49.
Find the cube root of $8000$.
50.
Evaluate the cube root: $\sqrt[3]{-216}$.
51.
Find the cube root of the decimal number $0.027$.
52.
Evaluate the cube root of the fraction: $\sqrt[3]{\frac{64}{343}}$.
53.
If the volume of a solid cubical box is $1000\text{ cm}^3$, find the length of its edge.
54.
Evaluate the sum: $\sqrt[3]{8} + \sqrt[3]{27}$.
55.
Evaluate the product: $\sqrt[3]{125} \times \sqrt[3]{64}$.
56.

57.

58.

59.
Find the value of $\sqrt[3]{0.008}$.
60.

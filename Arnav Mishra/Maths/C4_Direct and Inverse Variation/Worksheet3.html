Section A: Advanced Analytical Variation
1.
If $x$ and $y$ vary inversely, and $x = 25$ when $y = 3$, find $y$ when $x = 15$.
2.
If $x$ and $y$ vary inversely and constant of variation is 9, find $x$ when $y = 45$.
3.
Oranges cost ₹27 for 3 dozen. What is the cost of 8 oranges? (Analyze unit carefully).
4.
The extension of an elastic spring varies directly with weight. If 75kg produces 1.4cm, what weight produces 9.8cm?
5.
In 25 days, Earth picks up $6 \times 10^8$ lbs of dust. How much dust in 15 days?
6.
A car travels 60 km in 1 hr 30 min. How long for 100 km at same speed?
7.
Rachit drives at 30 km/hr and reaches in 4 hrs (9am to 1pm). By how much should he increase speed to reach by 12pm?
8.
A garrison of 120 men has food for 30 days. After 5 days, 5 more join. How many more days will the food last?
9.
If $x/y = k$, prove that $x^2/y^2$ also varies directly.
10.
A train 120m long at 50 km/hr passes a 130m platform. Find time in seconds.
Section B: Multi-Variable Challenges
11.
If 12 women weave 15m cloth in a day, how many women for 100m in a day?
12.
A labourer gets ₹675 for 9 days. How many days must he work to buy a ₹1500 item?
13.
A train 210m long took 12 seconds to pass a 90m tunnel. Find speed in km/hr.
14.
A train 270m long at 80 km/hr crosses a 130m platform. Calculate time precisely.
15.
If 10 students finish a report in 3 days, working 4 hrs/day, how many days for 15 students at 2 hrs/day?
Section C: Pattern Logic (HOTS)
16.
Identify Variation: $x$: [12, 3, 27, 6, 2]; $y$: [9, 36, 4, 18, 54].
17.
Identify Variation: $x$: [4, 20, 30, 40]; $y$: [2, 8, 10, 15].
18.
If $x$ varies directly as $y$, and $y$ varies inversely as $z$, what is the relation between $x$ and $z$?
19.
A pump empties $3/4$ of a tank in 15 mins. How long for the remaining?
20.
A bus speed increases from 40 to 60. By what percentage does the time taken decrease for the same distance?

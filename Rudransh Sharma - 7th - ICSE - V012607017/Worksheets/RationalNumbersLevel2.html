Standard Worksheet - Rational Numbers
Especially designed for: Rudransh Sharma Class: 7 (ICSE) Subject: Mathematics
Section A: Standard Form & Comparison
1.
Convert the rational number $\frac{64}{-112}$ to its standard form.
2.
Express $\frac{-78}{117}$ in its simplest form.
3.
Compare using $<$, $>$, or $=$: $\frac{-5}{9}$ ____ $\frac{4}{-7}$.
4.

5.
Find one rational number exactly between $\frac{-1}{3}$ and $\frac{1}{2}$.
6.
Find the value of $x$ if $\frac{-3}{7} = \frac{x}{35}$.
7.
Write an equivalent rational number for $\frac{-4}{5}$ with a numerator of $24$.
8.
Evaluate the absolute value: $\left| \frac{-5}{7} - \frac{2}{3} \right|$.
9.
Compare the absolute values: $\left| \frac{-3}{4} \right|$ ____ $\left| \frac{-5}{6} \right|$.
10.
Are the rational numbers $\frac{-16}{20}$ and $\frac{20}{-25}$ equivalent? Prove it.
11.
Insert three rational numbers between $\frac{-2}{5}$ and $\frac{-1}{5}$.
12.
Arrange in descending order: $\frac{2}{-3}$, $\frac{-4}{5}$, $\frac{-1}{2}$.
13.
Which is strictly smaller: $\frac{-8}{15}$ or $\frac{-7}{12}$?
14.
Express $\frac{-12}{13}$ as a rational number with a denominator of $-65$.
15.
Does every rational number have a multiplicative inverse (reciprocal)? Justify.

Section D: Real-Life Applications & Word Problems
46.
A rope of length $13\frac{1}{2} \text{ m}$ has been divided into $9$ pieces of equal length. What is the length of each piece?
47.
The area of a rectangular field is $45\frac{5}{16} \text{ sq m}$. If one side is $7\frac{1}{4} \text{ m}$, find the length of the other side.
48.
If $1 \text{ meter}$ of cloth costs ₹$35\frac{1}{2}$, find the cost of $4\frac{2}{5} \text{ meters}$ of the cloth.
49.
A train covers a distance of $120\frac{4}{5} \text{ km}$ in $3 \text{ hours}$. Find the speed of the train in km/h.
50.
Out of a $500$-page book, Ram read $\frac{3}{5}$ of the book on Monday and $\frac{1}{4}$ of the *remaining* pages on Tuesday. How many pages are still left to be read?
51.
Half of a herd of deer are grazing in the field, and $\frac{3}{4}$ of the remaining are playing nearby. The rest $9$ are drinking water from the pond. Find the total number of deer in the herd.
52.
Divide the sum of $\frac{65}{12}$ and $\frac{8}{3}$ by their difference.
53.
Simplify using property: $\frac{-2}{3} \times \frac{4}{5} + \frac{2}{3} \times \frac{1}{5}$.
54.
What is the perimeter of a rectangle with length $\frac{11}{4} \text{ cm}$ and width $\frac{7}{2} \text{ cm}$?
55.
If $\frac{a}{b}$ is a rational number, what fundamental mathematical condition must the integer $b$ satisfy?
56.
Find the multiplicative inverse of the sum of $\frac{1}{4}$ and $\frac{1}{5}$.
57.
Subtract the additive inverse of $\frac{5}{8}$ from the multiplicative inverse of $\frac{-4}{7}$.
58.
Evaluate $|x + y|$ if $x = \frac{-2}{5}$ and $y = \frac{1}{3}$.
59.
Is $\left( \frac{1}{2} - \frac{1}{3} \right) - \frac{1}{4}$ equal to $\frac{1}{2} - \left( \frac{1}{3} - \frac{1}{4} \right)$? What property does this test?
60.
A tailor needs $\frac{3}{4} \text{ m}$ of cloth to make a shirt. How many such shirts can he make from $15 \text{ m}$ of cloth?

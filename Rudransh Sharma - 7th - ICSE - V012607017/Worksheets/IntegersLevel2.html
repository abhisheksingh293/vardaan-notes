Standard Worksheet - Integers (Level 2)
Especially designed for: Rudransh Sharma Class: 7 (ICSE) Subject: Mathematics
Section A: Properties & Absolute Value
1.
Evaluate the given expression: $|-40| - |-12| + |-8|$.
2.
Write the integer which is $8$ less than the additive inverse of $-15$.
3.
Verify the commutative property of addition for $a = -14$ and $b = 9$.
4.
Name the property used here: $(-8) \times [ (-4) + 6 ] = [ (-8) \times (-4) ] + [ (-8) \times 6 ]$.
5.
Simplify using suitable properties: $26 \times (-48) + (-48) \times (-36)$.
6.
Simplify using suitable properties: $8 \times 53 \times (-125)$.
7.
Verify associative property of multiplication for $a = -2, b = 3, c = -4$.
8.
Is subtraction of integers associative? Prove with an example where $a=5, b=-3, c=2$.
9.
Evaluate: $-|-25| \times |-4|$.
10.
State the multiplicative identity for integers.
11.
Find the integer $x$ such that $x \times (-1) = 45$.
12.
Find the integer $y$ such that $y \div (-1) = -28$.
13.
Fill in the blank using properties: $(-15) + [ 8 + (-6) ] = [ (-15) + \_\_\_\_ ] + (-6)$.
14.
Which is greater: $(-12) - (-8)$ or $(-12) + (-8)$?
15.
Write a pair of negative integers whose difference gives $8$.

Section D: Real-Life Applications & Word Problems
46.
In a class test containing $15$ questions, $4$ marks are given for every correct answer and $-2$ marks are given for every incorrect answer. If Rohan attempts all questions but only gets $9$ correct, what is his total score?
47.
A certain freezing process requires that room temperature be lowered from $40^\circ\text{C}$ at the rate of $5^\circ\text{C}$ every hour. What will be the room temperature $10$ hours after the process begins?
48.
An elevator descends into a mine shaft at the rate of $6 \text{ m/min}$. If the descent starts from $20 \text{ m}$ above the ground level, how long will it take to reach a depth of $-340 \text{ m}$?
49.
A cement company earns a profit of ₹$8$ per bag of white cement sold and a loss of ₹$5$ per bag of grey cement sold. If they sell $3000$ bags of white cement and $5000$ bags of grey cement, what is their net profit or loss?
50.
The temperature at 12 noon was $10^\circ\text{C}$ above zero. If it decreases at the rate of $2^\circ\text{C}$ per hour until midnight, at what time would the temperature be $8^\circ\text{C}$ below zero?
51.
Mt. Everest is at an elevation of $8848 \text{ m}$ above sea level and the Mariana Trench is at an elevation of $-10994 \text{ m}$. Find the exact vertical difference between the two points.
52.
A submarine is floating at a depth of $1200 \text{ m}$ below sea level. If it ascends $250 \text{ m}$, what is its new depth?
53.
On Monday, the temperature in Leh was $-5^\circ\text{C}$. On Tuesday, it dropped by $2^\circ\text{C}$. On Wednesday, it rose by $4^\circ\text{C}$. What was the temperature on Wednesday?
54.
In a quiz, Team A scored $-40, 10, 0$ and Team B scored $10, 0, -40$ in three successive rounds. Which team scored more? Can we say integers can be added in any order?
55.
A shopkeeper earns a profit of ₹$2$ by selling one pen and incurs a loss of $50 \text{ paise}$ per pencil. If his net loss in a month is ₹$5$ and he sold $45$ pens, how many pencils did he sell?
56.
Suppose we represent the distance above the ground by a positive integer and below the ground by a negative integer. If an insect climbs $15 \text{ cm}$ up a pipe, slips down $20 \text{ cm}$, and then climbs up $8 \text{ cm}$, what is its final position relative to the start?
57.
Find the product of the predecessor of $-49$ and the successor of $-101$.
58.
Is the product of two negative integers and three positive integers positive or negative?
59.
A bank charges ₹$15$ for every bounced cheque. If a businessman had $4$ bounced cheques, and his initial balance was ₹$100$, what is his new balance after the penalty charges?
60.
Determine the integer which when divided by $-15$ gives a quotient of $-6$.
